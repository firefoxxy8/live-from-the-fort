About The Project

Live From The Fort is an online video music series by Vermont Public Radio. The intimate performances, recorded at VPR studios in Colchester's historic Fort Ethan Allen, provide an opportunity to discover and explore the rich and diverse contemporary music scene Vermont.

The series seeks musicians from throughout the state working in a variety of musical genres. Through the music and casual conversation, the series connects music lovers with music makers in Vermont.
